About Pastor Benedict Gbande Gbate

Servant of God, Leader, Teacher, and Friend

Biography

"To know Christ and make Him known"

Pastor Benedict Gbande Gbate was born and raised in Tatale City, where he gave his life to Christ at the age of 16. After hearing God's call to ministry during a youth retreat, he pursued theological education with passion and dedication.

He earned his Bachelor of Theology from Theological And Missions Seminary North Campus Kumbungu Tamale.

Ministry Journey

Pastor Benedict Gbande Gbate his ministry as a youth pastor in 1998, serving for five years before being called as an associate pastor at Grace Community Church. In 2005, he and his wife Sarah felt God's calling to plant a new church, which became Living Waters Ministry.

What started as a small Bible study group of 15 people has grown into a vibrant congregation of over 2,000 members, with multiple campuses and a strong online presence reaching people worldwide.

Family Life

Pastor John is happily married to Sarah Smith, his high school sweetheart and partner in ministry for 25 years. Together they have three children: Joshua (22), Hannah (19), and Samuel (16). The Smith family believes in serving together and can often be found volunteering in various church ministries.

"My greatest joy is seeing people transformed by the power of God's Word and walking in the purpose He has for their lives."

— Pastor John Smith
